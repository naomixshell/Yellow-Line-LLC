Bridging markets between Japan and South Asia

About Yellow Line LLC

Founded in November 2024, Yellow Line LLC specializes in cross-border trade between Japan and South Asian countries, focusing on two core business lines:

  1. Export of Japanese health supplements and tea products to Nepal and Bangladesh
  2. Import of Nepalese food products to Japan

With our headquarters in Tokyo's Kita-ku district, we leverage our founder's 13 years of experience in international trade to create efficient supply chains.
